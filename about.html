ABOUT THIS PROJECT

ABOUT DIL

DIL Daily Intent Lab is a focused training workspace for planning exercises, tracking streaks, and staying consistent with clear daily actions.

What You Can Do

CORE FLOW

Use one place to plan, execute, and review your training day.

  • Add and manage training exercises with structure.
  • Track completion streaks and momentum every day.
  • Use session mode for timed focus blocks.
  • Review movement guidance in the full training guide.

Design Intent

HOW IT FEELS

The interface is intentionally clear, fast, and motivating so users can focus on action instead of clutter.

  • Bold typography and direct language for quick decisions.
  • Progress-first cards that highlight what matters now.
  • Smooth reveal motion and cursor interactions for feedback.
  • A practical layout that works on desktop and mobile.

Step 1

Set your daily exercises and tasks on Training Board.

Step 2

Run Session Mode and complete items with consistency.

Step 3

Review dashboard trends and adjust tomorrow's plan.
